The Honest Answer

WHY BECOMING ME?

There are dozens of fitness apps out there. Here's why I built this one — and why it's different in ways that actually matter.

🇮🇳
Built for Indians, not adapted for them

Most fitness apps are designed for Western diets and gym cultures. I built Becoming Me from the ground up around Indian meals — dal, roti, paneer, poha — mapped to real macros. No substitution charts, no guessing. Your food, your goals.

🎯
No gym? No problem — ever

Apps like Nike Training or MyFitnessPal work great if you have equipment and a gym membership. Half of India works out at home or travels frequently. Becoming Me gives you a fully featured home workout mode — same plans, same quality, zero equipment needed.

💰
Completely free. No paywalls. No ads

Premium apps charge ₹800–₹3000/month. I built this as a passion project — not a business. Every single feature is free, forever. No "Pro" gate, no annoying upsells, no ads, no data selling. Just a tool that works for you.

⚡
Everything in one place — actually

Most people use 3–4 apps: one for workouts, one for diet, one for macros, one for streaks. Becoming Me is all of it in a single progressive web app you can install on your phone — workouts, Indian nutrition, macro calculator, calorie scanner, progress tracking, streaks, and achievements. One tab. One place.

🧠
Made by someone on the same journey

I'm not a corporation or a team of 50. I'm a developer who trains, struggled with the same frustrating apps, and decided to build something better. This app evolves because I use it myself. Your feedback reaches me directly — not a support ticket queue.

Still not sure? That's fine. Just try it — it takes 30 seconds to get started and costs absolutely nothing.
